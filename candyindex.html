Candy Corn

Interesting Facts

    Candy corn first appeared when the United States was a largely agrarian society,
    and its tri-color design was considered revolutionary. Lack of automated machinery
    meant that candy corn was only made seasonally, likely starting production in late
    August and continuing through the fall.

Nutritional Information

    Candy corn contains 150 calories per 40 g serving.
    This serving contains 0 g of fat, 0 g of protein and
    36 g of carbohydrate. The latter is 30 g sugar and 0 g
    of dietary fiber, the rest is complex carbohydrate.
    Candy corn contains 0 g of saturated fat and 0 mg of cholesterol per serving.
